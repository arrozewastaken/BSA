## __Intro__


> ## __Shredders in build a boat consist of a few main parts:__
>
> - The mouse guidance system and piston organisation.
> - The propulsion system and extra op tech (fling, etc).
> - The blades and design.
> - Setting up the shredder.

> ## __The mouse guidance system.__ 
>
> - ALL modern shredders use cannon welding to make shredders point towards the mouse.
> - This not only allows versatile manouverability, but also opens the pathway to an insane amount of technology.
> - To understand how to make basic mouse guiding, see: https://discord.com/channels/1463979497757085708/1472311792385654944
> - If you need screen shots on how to add them to shredders, see: https://discord.com/channels/1463979497757085708/1486480023035707402

> ## __Piston organisation.__ 
>
> - To make proper aesthetic design and armouring, using pistons to move parts of a shredder away from each other is a smart idea.
> - https://discord.com/channels/1463979497757085708/1486479595170435303

> ## __The propulsion system + tech.__ 
>
> - Previously, shredders used duped activated jets with spring propulsion for skybounce (turning without losing speed).
> - Newer designs used duped thrusters cloned into a part that is then pushed into the shredders core via pistons.
> - To dupe thrusters, have a part with an unanchored hinge + thrusters attached to it. Activate the thrusters, anchor them, and clone them. (https://discord.com/channels/1463979497757085708/1485078116518072400)
> - Some advanced shredders have fling systems, freeze blades or even crash blades. These abuse spring propulsion methods to achieve these.
> - See: https://discord.com/channels/1463979497757085708/1484684178657251439 https://discord.com/channels/1463979497757085708/1465735072228573453 https://discord.com/channels/1463979497757085708/1482656246384300114
> - Some shredders use magnet engines such as: https://discord.com/channels/1463979497757085708/1472312865313521705
>
> - This technology is what makes shredders *so* powerful in babft pvp, including the fact their fast AF.
