> ## _Shredders in build a boat consist of a few main parts:__
>
> - The mouse guidance system and piston organisation.
> - The propulsion system and extra (fling, etc).
> - The blades and design.

> ## __The mouse guidance system.__ 
>
> - ALL modern shredders use cannon welding to make shredders point towards the mouse.
> - This not only allows versatile manouverability, but also opens the pathway to an insane amount of technology.
